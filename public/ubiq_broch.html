Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who will own the charging stations?
    Ubiqpower retains ownership throughout the 5-year agreement. Upon expiry, the strata or property owner may exercise an option to purchase the units or extend the agreement.
  • Who is responsible for the electrical bill?
    Electricity costs are managed through the strata corporation or property owner and billed via BC Hydro, with Ubiqpower responsible for those charges.
  • Is approval guaranteed?
    No. Acceptance into the Zero Cost EV Charger Program is subject to UbiqPower's site review and approval.
  • How many chargers can a property receive?
    Ubiqpower holds exclusive authority to determine charger allocation per property, based on electrical capacity, EV driver demand, and minimum installation thresholds.
  • Is an upgrade to faster DC chargers possible?
    Yes. Ubiqpower may upgrade AC L2 units to DC L3 chargers — or to faster DC L3 units — where commercially viable and by mutual written agreement. Upgrades require a 480V EV-Ready connection.
